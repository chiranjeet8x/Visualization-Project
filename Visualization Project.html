Basic Plots

1. Bar Plot: Show total units sold by each product.

2. Pie Chart: Show percentage of total revenue by region.

3. Line Plot: Show total monthly revenue (month vs revenue).

4. Histogram: Show distribution of unit prices.

5. Box Plot: Compare unit prices for each product.

Grouped or Advanced Visualizations

6. Grouped Bar Plot: Compare average revenue per product across regions.

7. Stacked Bar Chart: Show monthly sales broken down by region.

8. Heatmap: Show average revenue by region and product.

9. Scatter Plot: Visualize relationship between units sold and revenue.

10. Pair Plot: Analyze relationship between numeric features.

Trend Analysis and Filters

11. Plot total revenue per salesperson.

12. Show the top 3 products by revenue using a horizontal bar chart.

13. Plot the average unit price for each product over months.

14. Plot the revenue of a selected region over the months (e.g., "North").

15. Create a violin plot to compare revenue distribution per product.
